Our Roadmap

Building the innovation hub — one phase at a time.

Phase 1

Foundation (2026-2028)

Launch the AI Research Lab, establish partnerships with Kenyan universities, and begin the Fellowship Program pilot.

Phase 2

Growth (2029-2032)

Scale the Fellowship Program, launch the Startup Incubator, and expand partnerships to regional and global institutions.

Phase 3

Global Hub (2033+)

Establish Scire Research as a globally recognized innovation hub — influencing AI policy and commercializing research.
